# Metastability Inc.

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **Metastability Inc.** - a technology company specializing in artificial intelligence, software engineering, and full-stack infrastructure development.

## About Us

Metastability Inc. is a privately-held technology company headquartered in California, USA. We design, develop, and deploy enterprise-grade systems across artificial intelligence, financial technology, embedded systems, and distributed infrastructure. Our work spans the entire technology stack - from low-level systems programming and bare-metal infrastructure to modern web platforms and machine learning pipelines.

We operate on a principle of complete technical sovereignty: all systems are designed, engineered, and maintained in-house, without dependency on third-party cloud providers or external proprietary ecosystems. This approach ensures maximum control, security, performance, and long-term stability for the solutions we deliver.

## Mission

To advance the development of artificial intelligence and computational systems through rigorous engineering, deterministic architecture, and accessible technology. We are committed to producing reliable, transparent, and high-performance software that addresses real-world technical challenges across industry and research.
